Three Principles

Precision in, precision out

AI amplifies whatever you give it. Structured user stories (Definition of Ready), detailed design before code. Vague input = vague output. The first steps drive everything.

Engineer it, don't prompt it

Apply proven SE best practices: TDD, static analysis, shift-left. Catch issues in design, not in production. If it makes human teams reliable, it makes AI reliable.

AI reviews AI, human decides

Fresh adversarial instance reviews until convergence. Human approves at phase gates — in GitHub, on the actual lines — not in the weeds.
